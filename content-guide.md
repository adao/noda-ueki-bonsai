# Content Strategy Guide

### Service Descriptions

#### Ueki (植木) - Traditional Japanese Garden Trees
Ueki refers to the art of cultivating and maintaining trees for Japanese gardens. This ancient practice involves careful selection, pruning, and shaping of trees to create harmony within the landscape. Our Ueki services include:
- Tree selection and placement consultation
- Structural pruning and shaping (Niwaki techniques)
- Seasonal maintenance and care
- Integration with existing landscape design
- Species expertise: Pine, Maple, Cherry, and more

#### Bonsai (盆栽) - The Art of Miniature Trees
Bonsai is the Japanese art of growing and shaping miniature trees in containers. More than just small trees, Bonsai represents a philosophy of harmony, patience, and respect for nature. Our Bonsai services include:
- Custom Bonsai creation and styling
- Maintenance and care training
- Seasonal care services
- Repotting and root work
- Species selection consultation
- Display and presentation advice

#### Full Landscape Design
Comprehensive Japanese garden design services that blend traditional aesthetics with modern functionality:
- Initial consultation and site analysis
- Custom design plans incorporating Ueki, stone work, water features
- Plant selection and procurement
- Installation and project management
- Ongoing maintenance plans
