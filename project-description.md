# Project: Japanese Landscaping Business Website
Client: Noda Ueki and Bonsai

## Overview
The client, Noda Ueki and Bonsai, is a Japanese landscaping business specializing in Noda Ueki and Bonsai services. They require a new website to showcase their expertise and attract potential clients. The website should have a clean, modern design that reflects the Japanese aesthetic and the business's specialties.

## Website Structure
1. Home
2. Company
   - About Us
   - Contact
3. Services
4. Portfolio
5. Resources
   - Blog
   - FAQ
   - Bonsai Gallery
6. Testimonials

## Design Concepts
1. Zen Harmony
   - Color Palette: Deep forest green (#2B4F3A), warm beige (#D9C8B0), soft red (#B56D6D), off-white (#F6F6F6)
   - Fonts: Noto Serif JP (headings), Noto Sans JP (body text)
   - Font Sizes: H1 (48px), H2 (36px), H3 (24px), Body Text (18px), Quotes (22px), Buttons (20px)
   - Design Elements: Full-width hero image, bamboo texture background, minimalistic icons, masonry-style layout for portfolio and gallery

2. Traditional Elegance
   - Color Palette: Muted green (#4E6E5D), pale gold (#D9C8B0), deep red (#8F3D3D), cream (#F4EEE2)
   - Fonts: Shippori Mincho (headings), Roboto (body text)
   - Font Sizes: H1 (48px), H2 (36px), H3 (24px), Body Text (18px), Quotes (22px), Buttons (20px)
   - Design Elements: Traditional Japanese illustration hero image, paper texture background, traditional Japanese patterns, grid layout with hover effects

3. Modern Serenity
   - Color Palette: Teal (#2E7F7D), light gray (#E0E0E0), ochre (#C77E5E), white (#FFFFFF)
   - Fonts: Poppins (headings), Open Sans (body text)
   - Font Sizes: H1 (48px), H2 (36px), H3 (24px), Body Text (18px), Quotes (22px), Buttons (20px)
   - Design Elements: Minimalistic hero section with striking Bonsai tree image, geometrical pattern background, line-based icons, slider layout for portfolio and gallery

## Content Requirements
- Sensible and reasonable stock photos as placeholders for Japanese landscaping, Noda Ueki, and Bonsai (may or may not be eventually updated by the client)
- Detailed information about the business, its history, and the owner's expertise
- Descriptions of services offered, including the design process and maintenance
- Portfolio of completed projects with images and brief descriptions
- Testimonials from satisfied clients
- Blog articles about Japanese landscaping techniques, Noda Ueki, and Bonsai care
- Frequently asked questions and answers
- Bonsai Gallery: A gallery showcasing different Bonsai and Ueki concepts, with images and descriptions of the techniques used

## Functionality
- Responsive design for optimal viewing on various devices
- Contact form for inquiries and consultation requests
- Services page with sub-navigation or table of contents for easy access to specific services

## Additional Notes
- The website should be easy to navigate and visually appealing
- The design should evoke a sense of tranquility, harmony, and connection with nature
- The website should be optimized for search engines and load quickly
