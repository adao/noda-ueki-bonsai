# Our Services

## Ueki (植木) - Traditional Japanese Garden Trees

Ueki is the centuries-old Japanese art of cultivating, shaping, and maintaining trees specifically for garden landscapes. Our Ueki services honor these traditional techniques while adapting to your unique environment.

### What We Offer

**Tree Selection & Consultation**
- Expert guidance on species selection for your climate, soil, and aesthetic goals
- Placement strategy to create visual harmony and proper growing conditions
- Consideration of mature size, seasonal changes, and long-term care requirements

**Niwaki Pruning & Shaping**
- Traditional Japanese pruning techniques to reveal natural tree architecture
- Structural pruning to create elegant, balanced forms
- Cloud pruning (Tamamono) for pines and junipers
- Candling and needle plucking for pine health and density

**Installation & Transplanting**
- Professional installation following proper horticultural practices
- Mature tree transplanting with specialized equipment
- Soil preparation and amendment for optimal growth
- Staking and initial care protocols

**Seasonal Maintenance**
- Ongoing pruning and shaping to maintain form
- Fertilization programs tailored to species and season
- Pest and disease monitoring and treatment
- Winter protection for sensitive species

### Common Ueki Species We Work With

- **Japanese Black Pine (Kuromatsu)** - Symbol of longevity and resilience
- **Japanese White Pine (Goyo-matsu)** - Elegant, fine-needled variety
- **Japanese Maple (Momiji)** - Stunning fall color and delicate form
- **Flowering Cherry (Sakura)** - Spring blooms and graceful branching
- **Japanese Cedar (Sugi)** - Evergreen structure and texture

## Bonsai (盆栽) - The Art of Miniature Trees

Bonsai is more than small trees in pots - it's a living art form requiring patience, skill, and deep understanding of horticulture. Our Bonsai services range from beginner guidance to advanced styling.

### What We Offer

**Custom Bonsai Creation**
- Selecting and styling nursery stock into Bonsai
- Collecting yamadori (wild material) when appropriate
- Multi-year development plans for pre-Bonsai material
- Air-layering to create new Bonsai from existing trees

**Styling & Refinement**
- Traditional and contemporary styling techniques
- Wiring for branch placement and movement
- Jin and Shari (deadwood techniques) for character
- Refinement of established Bonsai collections

**Repotting & Root Work**
- Proper timing and technique for species-specific needs
- Root pruning to maintain health and proportion
- Pot selection and custom sourcing
- Soil blending for optimal drainage and nutrition

**Care & Maintenance Services**
- Daily care for collections (watering, feeding)
- Seasonal pruning and pinching
- Pest and disease management
- Overwintering protection for outdoor Bonsai

**Education & Training**
- One-on-one instruction in Bonsai basics
- Species-specific care workshops
- Technique demonstrations (wiring, pruning, repotting)
- Ongoing mentorship for serious students

### Bonsai Styles We Specialize In

- **Formal Upright (Chokkan)** - Straight, tapered trunk with balanced branches
- **Informal Upright (Moyogi)** - Natural curves and movement
- **Slanting (Shakan)** - Angled trunk suggesting wind or hillside growth
- **Cascade (Kengai)** - Dramatic downward growth
- **Semi-Cascade (Han-Kengai)** - Horizontal then downward flow
- **Literati (Bunjin)** - Minimalist, abstract form inspired by Chinese paintings

## Complete Japanese Garden Design

Beyond individual trees, we create holistic Japanese garden spaces that embody traditional principles while functioning beautifully in modern settings.

### Our Design Process

**1. Initial Consultation**
- Site visit and assessment
- Discussion of your vision, goals, and budget
- Style exploration (traditional, contemporary, fusion)
- Preliminary recommendations

**2. Concept Development**
- Detailed site analysis (sun, shade, drainage, views)
- Conceptual design drawings or renderings
- Plant palette and material selection
- Budget refinement and timeline

**3. Detailed Design**
- Comprehensive planting plans
- Hardscape specifications (paths, stones, water features)
- Lighting design if requested
- Maintenance plan and seasonal care calendar

**4. Installation**
- Professional implementation by our experienced team
- Quality materials and plants
- Project management from start to completion
- Final walkthrough and care instructions

**5. Ongoing Support**
- Seasonal maintenance services available
- Plant replacement warranty
- Design adjustments as garden matures
- Long-term relationship with your evolving garden

## Design Principles We Honor

Our work is guided by timeless Japanese aesthetic principles:

- **Asymmetry (Fukinsei)** - Natural, unforced balance
- **Simplicity (Kanso)** - Restraint and clarity
- **Natural (Shizen)** - Effortless, not artificial
- **Subtle Depth (Yugen)** - Mystery and contemplation
- **Freedom from Attachment (Datsuzoku)** - Creativity within tradition
- **Tranquility (Seijaku)** - Peace and quiet energy
- **Imperfection (Wabi-Sabi)** - Beauty in impermanence and imperfection
